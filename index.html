Deflationary Liquidity Sistem

60% of the supply has been sent to the Dead Adress at launch. And the 100% of the Liquidity Pool Tokens are burned, this means that every transaction will be taxed with a 0.17% and asigned to the Dead Wallet. As holders add liquidity to the pool the taxation will be shared between the Dead Adress and the liquidity providers. This creates a high deflationary sistem in the first steps with decreasing burning rate over time and assures a rising price floor.

HOW TO GET POLYHEDRON

If you are an experienced BSC & Pancakeswap trader, you know how to get POLYHEDRON, for the rest this are the steps.

  • Purchase BNB on Binance, KuCoin or any other exchange of your choice.
  • Conect your Metamask to the Binance Smart Chain. Transfer your BNB to your MetaMask wallet.
  • Open Pancakeswap & accept the pop-up dialog to view the swapping interface.
  • Click the Gear icon in the top right of the exchange interface and change the slippage to at least 5%.
  • Enter the amount of Polyhedron you wish to buy with, click the Swap button, and then approve the transaction in MetaMask.
